Explanation
Class Details

Your Information
  • Shows your name, email, and your current skill level for this class.
  • Your class skill level determines which tasks you receive. The higher your skill level, the more challenging your tasks will be.
  • Your skill level increases when you answer questions correctly and can decrease if you answer many incorrectly.
  • There is a status shown for you in this class. Click on the status to get more information and a detailed AI analysis of your learning progress.
  • Privacy: Your teacher normally only sees what percentage of questions you answered correctly in each homework, but not which exact questions you got wrong or what your answers were.
    Exception: If you are at risk (skill level 3 or below and less than 50% of homework completed), your teacher can see exactly which questions you got wrong and what you answered. This helps your teacher to give you targeted support and help you improve.
Class Members
  • Lists all students in this class.
  • Next to each name, you see their favorite badges (up to 3), which show their achievements.
Task Completion
  • The bar shows how many homework assignments you have completed out of the total assigned in this class.
  • Each segment represents one homework. Completed segments are colored, unfinished ones are gray.
Team Challenges
  • Team Challenges are collaborative assignments where the whole class works together towards a common goal.
  • You see the goal score and the current score. When the goal is reached, the challenge is completed for everyone who participated in the challange.
  • Status shows whether the challenge is still open or already done.
Homework
  • Lists all individual homework assignments for this class.
  • Status shows whether you have completed the homework ("Done") or if it is still open.
  • Click on a homework or team challenge to view and work on the assignment.
Progress Over Time
  • The left chart shows your percentage of correct answers for each homework over time.
  • The right chart shows how your skill level in this class has changed over time.
  • Hover over the points in the charts to see details for each homework.
Leave Class
  • You can leave the class at any time by clicking the "Leave Class" button at the bottom of the page.
