Create Learning Content

Title & Description
  • Title: Give your assignment a clear and concise name.
  • Description: This field is sent to an AI that generates the tasks for you. The description should only specify the topic or general subject area for the tasks (e.g., "Photosynthesis in plants" or "Linear equations").
    The format and number of questions are already fixed: 30 multiple choice questions (10 per skill level Group) or 18 open questions (6 per skill level Group).
  • You can also import text from a PDF to the description field using the "Import Context from PDF" button.
Content Types
  • Multiple Choice: Students answer questions by selecting one of several options. You define the correct answer (index 0 = first option, 1 = second, etc.).
  • Open Question: Students write their own answers. You provide a sample solution, which is used by the AI to automatically check and evaluate student answers.
  • Team Challenge: If selected, the assignment becomes a collaborative challenge for the whole class. The class works together towards a common goal (goal score). This is designed to motivate students and foster teamwork.
AI Evaluation
  • For open questions, the AI compares student answers to your sample solution and decides if the answer is correct or not.
  • For multiple choice, the system automatically checks if the selected answer matches the correct index.
Skill Levels
  • Questions are assigned to students based on their skill level (1–10). This ensures that each student receives tasks appropriate for their abilities.
General Workflow
  • Fill in the title and description to give context to your assignment.
  • Choose the content type and fill in the required fields.
  • For team challenges, set a start/end time and a goal score.
  • Click "Create" to add the assignment to your class.
